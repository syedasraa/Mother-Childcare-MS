"The Mother & Childcare Management System is designed to help both individual patients and doctors easily access, operate, and compute details related to a mother and her child."

"For a normal pregnancy, our software will send automated notifications and reminders at specific dates for clinical appointments,

(such as medical check-ups, follow-up visits, and tests and scans). These reminders will help keep track of the development of the fetus, delivery date, and other important dates. In case of danger signs, our doctors can be directly contacted for support and assistance."
