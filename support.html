Pregnancy Care

We offer a range of resources and services to support you during your pregnancy, including:

  • Prenatal care appointments with a healthcare provider
  • Nutrition and exercise guidance
  • Information on childbirth and parenting classes
  • Emotional support and counseling

Mother and Childcare Management

We provide ongoing support for mothers and caregivers, including:

  • Well-baby checkups and immunizations
  • Breastfeeding support and resources
  • Information on child development and parenting
  • Referral to community resources and support groups
